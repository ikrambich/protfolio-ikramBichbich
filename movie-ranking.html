My Best Movies For 2023:

Let's pick my best 3 movies only ....Let's get started.

Ice and Fire

I love this movie because it shows that love can make the impossible possible even if the water and fire had to meet all together.

Saw

Unlike other horror movies this one takes in the entire scene of the detectives trying to catch the murder while you're trying to figure out what is going on.

Mouse

The cast , the story and mostly the direction with a matching bgm has made the drama just unbeatable and historic. From its opening scene it will make your heart pounding, you are in for a thrill ride that keeps up its psychological horror and identity crisis until the final reveal/plot twist.
